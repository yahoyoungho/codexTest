# Addicting HTML/CSS/JS Game — Concept Menu

You don’t need a concept yet — here are strong options you can prototype quickly.

## Quick Pick (Best Starting Option)

### 1) **Neon Drift** (Recommended)
A one-button lane-switch survival game.

- You move between 3 lanes.
- Obstacles spawn faster over time.
- Near-miss bonus rewards risky play.
- Rounds last 30–90 seconds.
- "One more try" comes from beating your best score by a tiny margin.

Why this is a great first build:
- Very simple controls (keyboard + touch).
- Easy to polish with juice (screen shake, glow trails, combo text).
- Easy difficulty tuning.

## 6 More Addicting Concepts

### 2) **Tap Forge**
Timing game where you tap to hit a moving sweet spot and forge items.
- Perfect timing builds combo multipliers.
- Misses break streak.
- Unlock visual upgrades and new materials.

### 3) **Orbit Collector**
You control a dot orbiting a core; tap to flip orbit direction and collect shards.
- Avoid incoming hazards.
- Chain collections for score bursts.
- Fast sessions with high skill ceiling.

### 4) **Merge Swarm**
Mini merge board where units auto-fight waves.
- Drag to merge quickly between waves.
- Build synergies and survive longer rounds.
- Great for progression + unlocks.

### 5) **Bounce Lab**
You place temporary bumpers to keep a ball alive in an arena.
- Physics chaos creates fun unpredictability.
- Limited bumper energy forces decisions.
- Great for streamer-friendly moments.

### 6) **Risk Ladder**
Choice-based score game: cash out now or continue harder challenges.
- Every success increases multiplier.
- One failure resets round score.
- Strong tension loop = high replayability.

### 7) **Pixel Heist**
Stealth-lite micro game: grab loot and escape before patrol patterns trap you.
- Levels are short and procedural.
- Stars/grades push mastery.
- Great for daily challenge mode later.

## Recommended Build Order (for any concept)

1. Build a playable loop in one HTML file.
2. Add score + restart + best score (`localStorage`).
3. Add feedback juice (particles, hit flash, combo text, tiny shake).
4. Add progression (unlock skins/modifiers).
5. Add one social hook (daily seed or share score text).
